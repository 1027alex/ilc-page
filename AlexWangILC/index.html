About Me

My name is Alex Wang, and I'm a second year at the University of Chicago. I'm from Bethesda, Maryland, and I'm interested in pursuing a career in business or marketing. This is my first year in ILC.

One idea I came up with recently is Ikea Paper. During the quarantine, Ikea developed an app that uses augmented reality (AR) technology to allow people to see what a virtual mockup of a piece of furniture would look like in their home. However, one of the biggest issues with AR furniture shopping is that it is very difficult for the AR to correctly size items. Ikea Paper would be a small piece of paper covered in QR code that could be placed near the object as a scaling tool. It would provide a more consistent reference point, instead of requiring the software to try to guess the dimensions of the room from its contents.
